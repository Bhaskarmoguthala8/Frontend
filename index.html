About The Rambling House Bar & Restaurant

Established in 2024, The Rambling House Bar & Restaurant brings the warmth and charm of traditional Irish hospitality to Laghey, Donegal. Our cosy pub and restaurant celebrate the flavours of Donegal, with a menu showcasing local ingredients. Located just off the N15, we're close to breathtaking beaches and golf courses, making us the perfect stop for locals and visitors alike.
